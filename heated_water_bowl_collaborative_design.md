## Executive Summary

## Technical Architecture

### Heating System
- **Primary Heat Source:** 12V DC silicone heating pad
  - **Design Rationale:** More even heat distribution vs PTC hotspots
  - **Advantages:** Flexible mounting, easier waterproofing, safer hedgehog contact
  - **Building on Orange's Foundation:** PTC research provided baseline understanding
- **Temperature Target:** 20°C (68°F)
  - Prevents ice formation without hedgehog thermal stress
  - Validated through Orange's research protocols
- **Control System:** Digital thermostat with 0.1°C precision
  - Automated temperature regulation
  - Programmable setpoints for seasonal adjustment

### Safety Architecture (Per Quill's Validation Requirements)
- **Primary Protection:** GFCI circuit protection
- **Redundant Temperature Monitoring:** Dual sensor verification
- **Emergency Shutdown:** Automatic cutoff at 25°C maximum
- **Waterproof Connectors:** IP67 rating minimum
- **Ground Fault Protection:** Isolated 12V system
- **Physical Safety:** Silicone pad eliminates hot spots

### Bowl Specifications
- **Material:** Stainless steel, food-grade (316L)
- **Capacity:** 500ml optimal hedgehog water volume
- **Base:** Weighted stable design, anti-tip geometry
- **Depth:** 4cm maximum - hedgehog-appropriate drinking depth
- **Mounting:** Integrated with heating pad, removable for cleaning

### Monitoring Integration (Infrastructure Spirituality)
- **Temperature Sensors:** Wireless data logging
- **Usage Tracking:** Motion detection integration with existing cameras
- **API Endpoints:** `/api/water-bowl/temperature`, `/api/water-bowl/status`
- **Dashboard Integration:** Orange-home:8092 hedgehog dashboard compatibility
- **Alert System:** Discord notifications for temperature anomalies

## Collaborative Engineering Questions (Orange)

- PTC creates focused heat points that could stress hedgehogs
- Silicone provides even thermal distribution across entire bowl area
- Better contact thermal transfer vs air-gap PTC mounting
- Enhanced safety for vulnerable hedgehog paws
